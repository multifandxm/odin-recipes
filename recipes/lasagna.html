Lasagna

Photo from Pixabay

Description

Garfields favourite dish made easy.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Cheeses: cottage cheese, mozzarella and Parmesan
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the beef, then stir in the sauce and let simmer
  2. Combine the cheeses, eggs and seasonings
  3. Assemble the lasagna
  4. Bake covered for 45 minutes
  5. Uncover and bake for 10 more minutes
  6. Serve and enjoy
