Potato Pancakes

Photo from Pixabay

Description

It's an easy and quick pancakes recipe with a twist!
Potato pancakes are both crispy and tender.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Egg
  • Seasonings
  • Flour
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Grate the potatoes and drain the excess liquid
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients in a bowl and mix
  3. Drop a spoonfull of the mix into hot oil, then flatten it with the back of a spatula
  4. Fry until they look golden
  5. Serve and enjoy!
