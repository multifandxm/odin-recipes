Description

Easy chocolate chips cookies.

Ingredients

  • Brown sugar
  • Softened butter
  • Eggs
  • All-purpose flour
  • Semi-sweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients and preheat the oven to 175 degrees C
  2. Beat brown sugar and butter
  3. Add eggs and flour gradually
  4. Add chocolate chips
  5. Drop dough in spoonfulls a few centimeters apart onto ungreased baking sheets
  6. Bake 10 to 15 minutes
  7. Let cool, serve and enjoy
